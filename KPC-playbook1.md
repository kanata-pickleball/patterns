# 🏓 KPC Pickleball Team Playbook  
## 4 Positions Rotation System (10 Minutes Each)

---

## 📋 Format

- 4 players per court  
- Each player rotates every **10 minutes**  
- All players practice the **same play at each station**  
- Focus: **consistency, teamwork, decision-making**

---

## 🔁 Rotation Order

1. **Server** (Baseline Right)  
2. **Server’s Partner** (Baseline Left → transition forward)  
3. **Returner** (Baseline Right)  
4. **Returner’s Partner** (Kitchen Line Left)  

➡️ After 10 minutes → rotate clockwise

---

# 📍 Position 1 — Server (Baseline Right)

## 🎯 Objective
Start the point safely and prepare to move forward.

## ✅ What to Do

### Before Serve
- Use a consistent pre-serve routine  
- Aim deep (middle or backhand preferred)  
- Call the serve target if practicing teamwork  

### After Serve
- Stay back initially  
- Watch return quality  
- Move forward only when safe (usually after the 3rd shot)

## 🔑 Key Focus
- High-percentage deep serves  
- No risky power serves  
- Ready for 3rd-shot decision  

## 🧠 Common Mistakes
- Serving short  
- Rushing to the kitchen too early  
- Watching the serve instead of preparing  

---

# 📍 Position 2 — Server’s Partner  
*(Starts baseline left → transitions to NVZ)*

## 🎯 Objective
Execute the **3rd-shot strategy** and lead the team forward.

## ✅ What to Do

### On Return Coming Back
- Decide: **3rd shot drop OR drive**  
- Prefer drop if under control  
- Communicate: “mine” or “yours”

### After 3rd Shot
- Move forward with partner  
- Split step near kitchen line  
- Prepare for soft game  

## 🔑 Key Focus
- High, soft drops into kitchen  
- Controlled drives when ball is high  
- Smooth transition forward  

## 🧠 Common Mistakes
- Driving every 3rd ball  
- Charging the net too early  
- Hitting drops too hard  

---

# 📍 Position 3 — Returner (Baseline Right)

## 🎯 Objective
Hit a deep return and get to the kitchen fast.

## ✅ What to Do

### On Return
- Hit deep crosscourt or middle  
- Add height for safety  
- Immediately move forward  

### Transition
- Sprint to NVZ line  
- Split step before opponent hits  
- Prepare for 4th shot  

## 🔑 Key Focus
- Deep, high returns  
- Quick but controlled transition  
- Balanced split-step timing  

## 🧠 Common Mistakes
- Returning short  
- Admiring the return  
- Running through the kitchen line  

---

# 📍 Position 4 — Returner’s Partner (Kitchen Left)

## 🎯 Objective
Control the soft game and apply pressure.

## ✅ What to Do

### During Opponent’s 3rd Shot
- Be ready to volley  
- Look for attackable balls  
- Keep paddle up  

### Soft Game
- Dink crosscourt primarily  
- Move opponent side to side  
- Reset hard balls into kitchen  

### When Ball Is High
- Speed up to opponent’s body or middle  
- Stay balanced after attack  

## 🔑 Key Focus
- Patient dinking  
- Smart speed-ups  
- Paddle-ready position  

## 🧠 Common Mistakes
- Attacking low balls  
- Dinking too high  
- Standing flat-footed  

---

# ⏱️ Suggested 40-Minute Session

- **Round 1 (10 min)** — Starting positions  
- **Round 2 (10 min)** — Rotate  
- **Round 3 (10 min)** — Rotate  
- **Round 4 (10 min)** — Rotate  

✅ Every player experiences every role  
✅ Repetition builds pattern memory  
✅ Easy for club organization  

---

# 🏆 Optional Coaching Emphasis (Advanced 3.5–4.0)

Add these constraints if desired:

- Serve target zones  
- 3rd-shot drop accuracy goals  
- Minimum dink rally (e.g., 6 shots before speed-up)  
- Communication calls (“switch”, “mine”, “bounce”)

---

**Tip:** Run this weekly to build strong doubles habits and team chemistry.
